Language: #lingo/spanish 
Status: #status/active 

## 🗺️ Learning Path (CEFR Levels)

### Phase 1: 01 - Beginner (A1-A2)
- [ ] **A1 (Acceso)**: Introductions, ordering food, basic questions.
  - *Key Grammar*: Subject pronouns (`yo`, `tú`), `Ser` vs. `Estar`, Regular present tense (`-ar`, `-er`, `-ir`).
- [ ] **A2 (Plataforma)**: Describing past experiences, simple storytelling.
  - *Key Grammar*: Preterite vs. Imperfect (Past), Reflexive verbs, Direct/Indirect object pronouns.

### Phase 2: 02 - Intermediate (B1-B2)
- [ ] **B1 (Umbral)**: Expressing opinions, future plans, conditional statements.
  - *Key Grammar*: Present Subjunctive, Future tense, Conditional.
- [ ] **B2 (Avanzado)**: Understanding complex themes, defending a viewpoint.
  - *Key Grammar*: Imperfect Subjunctive, Advanced compound tenses.

### Phase 3: 03 - Expert (C1-C2)
- [ ] **C1 (Dominio Operativo Eficaz)**: Using the language for social, academic, and professional purposes. Fine-tuning nuances.
- [ ] **C2 (Maestría)**: Near-native proficiency. Precision in complex situations.
