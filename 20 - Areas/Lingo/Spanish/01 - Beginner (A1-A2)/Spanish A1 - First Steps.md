| Por favor   | Please  | Café, por favor. |

## ⚖️ Grammar / Structure
- **Rule**: Nouns have two genders: Masculine (`o`) and Feminine (`a`).
- **Rule**: Sentence structure is usually S-V-O, but Spanish is flexible.
- **Rule**: Pronouns like "I" (`yo`) are often omitted if the verb conjugation is clear.

## 🗣️ Common Phrases & Idioms
- **¿Cómo te llamas?** (What's your name?)
- **Me llamo...** (My name is...)
- **¿Hablas inglés?** (Do you speak English?)

## 🧠 Questions & Flashcards
- **Q**: What are the two verbs for "to be" in Spanish?
  - **A**: `Ser` (permanent) and `Estar` (temporary).
