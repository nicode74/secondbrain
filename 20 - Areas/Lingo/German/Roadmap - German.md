# Roadmap: German Mastery (Deutsch)
Language: #lingo/german 
Status: #status/active 

## 🗺️ Learning Path (CEFR Levels)

### Phase 1: 01 - Beginner (A1-A2)
- [ ] **A1 (Breakthrough)**: Basic greetings, personal info, numbers, time, simple shopping.
  - *Key Grammar*: Definite/Indefinite articles (`der`, `die`, `das`), Verb conjugation (Present), Pronouns.
- [ ] **A2 (Waystage)**: Simple conversations about family, work, and surroundings.
  - *Key Grammar*: Past tense (`Perfekt`), Modal verbs (`können`, `müssen`), Accusative and Dative cases.

### Phase 2: 02 - Intermediate (B1-B2)
- [ ] **B1 (Threshold)**: Describing dreams, hopes, and events. Giving reasons and explanations.
  - *Key Grammar*: Passive voice, Subjunctive II (`Konjunktiv II`), Genitive case.
- [ ] **B2 (Vantage)**: Understanding complex technical discussions. Conversing fluently with native speakers.
  - *Key Grammar*: Complex sentence structures, Advanced prepositions.

### Phase 3: 03 - Expert (C1-C2)
- [ ] **C1 (Effective Operational Proficiency)**: Recognizing implicit meaning. Using the language flexibly for social, academic, and professional purposes.
- [ ] **C2 (Mastery)**: Expressing yourself spontaneously and fluently. Understanding almost everything heard or read.
