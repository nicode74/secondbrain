## ⚖️ Grammar / Structure
- **Rule**: Nouns are always capitalized in German.
- **Example**: `das Haus` (the house), `der Mann` (the man).
- **Rule**: Three genders for articles: Masculine (`der`), Feminine (`die`), Neuter (`das`).

## 🗣️ Common Phrases & Idioms
- **Wie heißt du?** (What is your name?)
- **Ich heiße...** (My name is...)
- **Woher kommst du?** (Where are you from?)
